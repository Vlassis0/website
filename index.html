Questions

Before you apply

Questions we get asked. If yours isn't here, reach out and ask.

Who qualifies for a free computer?

Path A is for people with zero income right now — job hunters, students, or on public assistance. We ask on the form so we can match you right. Be honest about your situation.

What is "cost assistance"?

You have a job, so we don't charge full price. You tell us what you can afford — no pay stubs, no proof — and we price it there. We offer payment plans too, so you're not stuck paying it all upfront.

What shape are these computers in?

Rebuilt, not used-as-is. Cleaned, tested for hours under load, repaired or replaced part-by-part. Every machine comes with 90 days of support. If something breaks, bring it back and we'll fix it.

Can I pick what kind of computer I get?

Tell us what you'll use it for — job applications, schoolwork, remote work — and we match a build to that. We work with what's donated, so specs vary, but it'll do what you need it to.

Can I donate a computer?

Yes. Email us at 805-801-5585 or call and we'll let you know what we can take and what happens next.
